GRU vs LSTM Under Memory Stress
1. Introduction
Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs) are designed for sequential data but suffer from 
vanishing and exploding gradients
. To overcome this, advanced architectures like 
GRU (Gated Recurrent Unit)
 and 
LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory)
 were introduced.
This project evaluates their 
memory retention capability under stress conditions
, i.e., when sequence lengths increase significantly.
2. Objective
